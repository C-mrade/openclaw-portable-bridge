# Code of conduct

We want this project to be a welcoming, practical, and technically rigorous
place to collaborate. Be respectful, assume good faith, critique ideas rather
than people, and make space for contributors with different backgrounds and
experience levels.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, and publication of private or
sensitive data are not acceptable. Maintainers may edit or remove content and
restrict participation when needed to protect contributors or users.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository's
private security reporting channel. Reports will be handled confidentially
and in good faith.
